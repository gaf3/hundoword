Students

Students are anyone who wants to learn to read, from your own children (Parents) to children you teach in class (Teachers).

Lessons

Lessons are collections of words early readers are expected to learn. The words vary but there is a great deal of overlap.

Achievements

Achievements are actions a Student is able to perform on a Word, such as matching, sight reading, word search, etc. Students can Attain an achievement and also Yield an achievement. This allows you to see words they're struggling with vs. words they know well.

Pick a Lesson

Select from several Lesson which words you want students to learn. You can also enter your owns if you feel they're important.

Play Games

Have your Students play games to determine what Achievements they're able to attain. As they play, words they Attain and Yield are recorded.

Gauge Performance

Get an overview on how your students are doing, from total words to learn, to which words they have down, to which activities they tend to perform well at.
